About me

My name is Dante, and I'm twenty years old. I'm a college student who wants to learn how to create websites. I enjoy playing video games, learning more about technology, and gaining new knowledge about HTML. Initially, I aim to create simple yet effective websites. As I improve my skills with HTML and other resources, I aspire to build more complex and polished websites in the future.

My interest in technology began at the age of 10 when my grandpa introduced me to his old computer and taught me how to play games. Those early experiences sparked my curiosity, and I eventually delved into coding. Now, as a computer science major, I'm excited to continue my journey and explore the world of coding and technology.
